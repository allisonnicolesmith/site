Born in Grand Prairie, Texas, I moved to New York City to study creative writing at CUNY Hunter College. I’ve taught English as an adjunct lecturer. I’ve worked in college textbook publishing as an assistant media editor. I also lived in Bangkok for a year, where I wrote on topics related to food and travel. Now, I'm a student at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY, where I’m learning how to not use the Oxford comma.

My reporting has appeared in Grub Street, Gotham Gazette, Travel + Leisure Southeast Asia, AFAR, Hidden Compass, DestinAsian and others.
